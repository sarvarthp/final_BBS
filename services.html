Circulations

-- Enter the name in the entry register kept at the circulation counter.

-- Home lending books and general books are issued to all eligible members.

-- Students are permitted to borrow four books for the period of fifteen days.

-- Borrowing of books can be made against the library card only.

-- In order to get the books renewed, they have to be physically presented at the circulation counter of the library.

-- Books are reissued only if there is no claim.

-- Reference books, bound volumes, journals are not lent out of the library.

-- Book in damaged condition will not be accepted from the reader.
